Story

In today's fast paced world, our thoughts, intentions and goals are often clouded. We find ourselves focusing solely on the end goal, without thinking much about the process of the goal. This can result to increased stress levels as we become unsure or even confused about why we are so focused on a certain goal or intention.

Solution

Our app, BlueSky helps people clarify their thoughts and intentions by asking the user questions about the intention. This promotes critical thinking and reflection and helps the user really think about the process of their intention, rather than simply focusing on the end goal. The user can go back to their previous intentions and read their answers. Having all of their answers in a single place helps the user be more mindful and aware of their own intentions and thoughts.
